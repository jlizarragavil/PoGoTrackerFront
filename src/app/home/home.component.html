Welcome to the Pokemon GO Tracker Application!

Here you can track various aspects of your gaming experience:

Features

PVP Log: Monitor and analyze your PVP battles, view detailed logs, and filter results based on your preferences.

XP Tracker: Keep track of your daily XP gains, see how much XP you need to reach level 50, and get insights into your overall progress.

Attackers Table: View a table of the most common attackers in PVP, including information on the number of basic moves required for their charged attacks.

Minigame: Engage in a fun minigame to learn about the number of basic moves needed for different Pokemon to reach a charged attack.
